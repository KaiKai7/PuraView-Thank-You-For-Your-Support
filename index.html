Thank you for supporting me through the usage of my app PuraView. I created this app when I realized that I am pretty good at tech stuff, but finding out what was in my water was hard even for me.

I looked for an app that could tell me, and discovered that there isn't one. And around this point I almost gave up, and I assume others would also.

So I spent countless hours figuring out how to make sense of all the data that was available. And then spent even more time creating PuraView.

Through trial and error, I think I have brought together much of what I originally sought after.

And that is a simple, easy way to know what was found in my drinking water.

I thought about making this app ad-supported to help me continue to develop this and other similar projects.

But doing all this work to make it easy to view your water quality, and then hindering the ability to view it, didn't make sense.

So instead of ads for everybody, I have decided to include no ads and make PuraView free for all.

A voluntary donation to Buy Me A Coffee can be made, which will help me continue to develop PuraView and allow PuraView to remain ad-free.

Thank you for your support!
